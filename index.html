Classic Cheesecake Recipe

Look no further for a creamy and ultra smooth classic cheesecake recipe! Paired
with a buttery graham cracker crust,no one can deny its simple decadence.For
the best results,bake in a water bath.

ingerdients

Graham Cracker Crust









3 Large eggs,at room temperature
topping suggesitions: salted caramel,lemon curd, strawberry
topping,chocolate ganache, red wine chocolate ganache, fresh


YIELD
12 servings

PREP TIME
45 minutes

COOK TIME
1 hour

TOTAL TIME
7,75 hours

Instructions

1 Adjust the oven rack to the lower-middle position and preheat oven to 350°F (177°C).

2 Make the crust : Using a food processor, pulse the graham
crackers into crumbs. Pour into a medium bowl and stir in sugar
and melted butter untill combined.(You can also pluse it all together in the
food processor.) Mixture will be sandy. Press firmly into the
bottom and slightly up the sides od a 9-inch or 10 inch
springform pan. No no need to grease the pan first.I use the bottom
of a mesuring cup to pock the crust down tight.Pre-break for 8
minutes.Remove from the oven and place the hot pan for the
water bath in step 4.Allows crust to slight cools as you prepare
the filling.

3 Make the filling : Using a handheld or stand mixer fitted with a
paddle attachment, beat the cream cheese and granulated sugar
together on mediun-high speed in a large bowl until the mixture
is smooth and cream ,about 2 minutes .Add the sour cream,
vanilla extract, and lemon juice then beat until fully combined .On
medium speen, add the egg once at athime , beating after each
addition until just blended .After the final egg is incorporated into
the batter ,stop mixing. To help prevent the cheesecakes from
deflating and cracxking as it cools, avoid over-mixing the batter
as best you can.

4 Prepare the simple water bath (see note) Boil the pot of water
You need 1 inch of water in your rosting pan for the water bath
so make sure you boil enough, I use an kettle of hot water
As the water is heating up, wrap the aluminium foil around the
springform pan.Pour the cheesecake batter on the top of the crust.
Use a rubber spatula or spoon to smooth in into an even layer .
Place the pan inside of the pan and place in the oven
first, then pour the hot water in.Whichever is easier for you.

5 Bake cheese cake for 55-70 minutes 0r untill the center is almost
set.When it's done, the center of the cheesecake will slightely
wobble if you gently shake the pan. Turn the oven off and open
the oven door slightly .let the cheesecake sit in the oven in the
water bath as it cools down for 1 hour.Remove from the oven and
water bath,then cool cheesecake completely at room
temperature .Then refrigerate the cheesecake for at least 4 hours
or overnight

6 Use aknife to loosen a chilled cheese cake from the rim of the
springform pan, then remove the rim.Using a clean sharpo knife,
cut into slices for serving .For neat slices,wipe the knife clean and
dip into warm between each slice.

7 serve cheesecake with desired toppings.Covers and store leftover
cheesecake in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.
